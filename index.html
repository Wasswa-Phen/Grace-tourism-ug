Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in tour packages?

Our packages typically include transportation, accommodation, meals, park fees, and professional guide services. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Do I need special permits?

Yes, gorilla trekking requires permits. We handle all permit bookings and documentation for our guests to ensure a smooth experience.

What's the best time to visit?

Uganda is great year-round! Dry seasons (Dec-Feb, Jun-Aug) are ideal for wildlife viewing, while wet seasons offer lush landscapes and fewer crowds.
